About Us

We Leave A Delicious Memory For You

Klassy Cafe is a pinnacle of years of planning, organisation and dedication located in Koramagala, the heart of the Garden city of India. Our team has aimed to create an opulent cafe with international standards all while standing true to its roots.

We are dedicated towards cultivating a minimal, relaxing, friendly and comfortable environment for our customers. Here they can enjoy luxurious yet affordable high quality food from breakfast all the way to dinner. Whether it is a sip of black coffee or a rich slice of cheesecake, we have food that makes your heart smile.
